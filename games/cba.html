About Space Waves

Space Waves is an exciting side-scrolling game that combines fast-paced action and challenges. Control an arrow to fly through space tunnels full of obstacles.

This game requires the player to control an arrow to avoid obstacles until you touch the end of each level. There are 33 levels of varying difficulty, categorized by emojis, allowing you to choose the level of challenge you want to face.

Space Waves was released in April 2024 by do.games and is a popular game among players today.

How to play

What is your goal?

You aim to dodge obstacles, destroy enemy spaceships, and collect power-ups to increase your score and survive as long as possible. Each level of Space Waves has a similar goal of completing the level without letting the arrows collide with walls, spinning gears, or spikes.

Countless obstacles

Space tunnels are endlessly generated, ensuring a unique and challenging experience every time you play. Along the way, obstacles will appear, such as walls, spikes, and spinning gears. It would help to control your arrow to avoid collisions with these obstacles.

Level of difficult

"Green" levels will have more space and more accessible angles, allowing you to increase the straight speed of your arrow. You can expect to encounter more obstacles, narrower edges, and more when you choose more difficult levels. You can choose any level you want to play, so you can start with the hardest one, which can quickly help you realize the need for quick reactions.

Enemy spaceship

Be careful because enemy spaceships will periodically appear and shoot at you. Destroy them to earn points and power-ups. Collect power-ups to enhance your spaceship's abilities, such as increasing firepower, temporary invulnerability, or slowing down the game speed.

How to control

  • You can use the left and right arrows on your device screen to move the spaceship horizontally.
  • To shoot, you can tap the screen to fire the spaceship's laser.
  • PC controls: Use the left mouse button / W key / Spacebar key to go up and release to go down.
  • Mobile controls: Tap the screen to go up and let go to go down.

Game modes

World Mode 1

World 1 mode is the first level in the Space Waves. This level usually has a lower difficulty, with more space and fewer obstacles, helping players get used to the game's controls and gameplay. World Mode 1 is the ideal mode for new players to start and learn to control their arrows accurately.

Endless Mode

Endless mode is a game mode with no specific endpoint, where you try to go as far as possible. In this mode, you will face constant obstacles and test your ability to react quickly. Endless is a great game mode to challenge yourself and see how quickly you can maintain focus and respond in a pressure-filled environment.

Both modes offer excellent opportunities to develop your gaming skills and strategies. World 1 mode helps you get used to the game, while Endless mode challenges you to achieve the highest score. Try both modes to see which suits you best and how you want to conquer Space Waves!

Tips and strategies to play Space Waves

You should play a lot if you want to be good at any game. The more you play, the easier it will be to master controlling the spacecraft and avoiding obstacles better.

  • Know how to use power-ups wisely and strategically to gain an advantage in difficult situations.
  • Along the way, many things will distract you, such as backgrounds or visual effects. Try to focus on obstacles and enemy spaceships on your way. Move in a zigzag pattern when obstacles are ahead; this will help you maintain straight momentum.
  • As you progress, you can upgrade your starship's statistics, such as speed, firepower, and power-up item duration.
